# Data Ethics & Responsible AI

LinguaNE is designed with ethical AI principles:

## Consent
- Explicit consent required for guest users
- Data is never stored without permission

## Community Ownership
- Feedback is validated by native speakers
- No automated overwrite of language data

## Transparency
- Mock models clearly identified
- Model confidence always exposed

## Cultural Sensitivity
- Languages treated as living heritage
- No monetization of user-submitted corrections
